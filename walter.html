Partner

His Story: Walter Rose acts as the catalyst for transactions and advises portfolio companies on key initiatives and strategic planning. Prior to founding Valencia Capital, LLC, Walter was President and CEO of Rostra Technologies, which he founded in 1982 for the purpose of acquiring manufacturing companies. Rostra Technologies acquired a number of companies under Walter’s direction, including International Foils, Gibraltar Packaging Group, Rostra Precision Controls, Rostra Tool and Rostra Vernatherm. In each case the acquired company was built up through organic growth and synergistic add-on acquisitions. Prior to founding Rostra Technologies, Walter ran major divisions and companies for ITT and Thomas Tilling Inc.

Wheelhouse: Finding and funding excellence.

Education: Walter Rose holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Florida and received his M.B.A. from New York University.

Extras: Walter is a connoisseur of German automobiles and French wines.
