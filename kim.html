Partner

Her Story: Kim Rose has over a decade of hands-on experience in advertising, marketing and production. Delivering top-flight services to private companies and agencies has lent Kim an insider’s perspective on how a variety of organizations function, while honing her ability to zero in on what matters most and identify key opportunities in record time.

Kim is currently the Director of Studio Operations for Tommy Hilfiger. Formerly, she served as the respective Production/Project Manager for brands as diverse as Cole Haan, Mercedes Benz and Wenner Media.

Wheelhouse: Being awesome and making awesome things happen.

Education: Kim holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Fordham University and a license to rock.

Extras: Kim loves writing songs, her pet turtle, guitars, and appreciates a well-crafted cheeseburger.
